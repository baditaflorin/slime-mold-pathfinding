# Privacy

V1 collects no analytics.

The app has:

- No accounts.
- No cookies created by the app.
- No runtime backend.
- No telemetry beacons.
- No third-party map tile requests.

Local browser state:

- The selected city may be stored in `localStorage`.
- The service worker may cache static app and city-data files for offline-friendly loading.

External links:

- Repository: https://github.com/baditaflorin/slime-mold-pathfinding
- PayPal support: https://www.paypal.com/paypalme/florinbadita

Those external sites have their own privacy policies.
